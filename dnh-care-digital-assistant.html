DNH Care Digital Assistant

DNH Care Digital Assistant is an internal automation tool built for and operated by Dr. Nafia's Homoeopathic Medical Centre (dnhcare.co.in), Varthur, Bengaluru. It is not a public product — it has no sign-up, no other users, and is used solely by the clinic's own team to manage the clinic's own online presence.

What it does

The assistant helps the clinic publish clinic-authored, doctor-reviewed content in two places:

  • New articles on the clinic's own website journal, dnhcare.co.in/blog
  • Local Business Profile posts on the clinic's own Google Business listing, so patients searching Google see up-to-date information from the clinic

Every piece of content is generated as a draft and reviewed by clinic staff before publication — nothing goes live automatically without a human approving it first.

What data it accesses

To post to the clinic's Google Business Profile and show the clinic a weekly summary of how the listing is performing, the assistant requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/business.manage scope, limited to:

  • Creating and removing posts on the clinic's own Business Profile listing
  • Reading the clinic's own listing performance (calls, direction requests, search impressions, and the search terms people used to find the clinic) and its own review ratings

It does not access any other business's data, any other Google user's data, or any data outside the clinic's own single Business Profile listing. See the privacy policy for full details.
