Homeopathic Care for Children

Children seem to live at the doctor's clinic in their early years — one cold rolls into the next, tonsils flare every few months, appetite disappears, a rash arrives without warning. At Dr. Nafia's Homoeopathic Medical Centre in Varthur, paediatric care is one of our core specialities: gentle, individualized treatment plans built around your child's age, temperament and history.

Common childhood complaints we see

  • Recurrent colds and coughs
  • Tonsillitis and throat complaints
  • Low appetite and fussy eating
  • Childhood skin complaints
  • Colic and digestive troubles
  • Sleep and behavioural concerns
  • Growth and immunity concerns

A gentle, child-appropriate approach

Homeopathic remedies are commonly used for children, and case-taking for a child looks very different from an adult consultation. Dr. Nafia observes how your child behaves, sleeps, eats and reacts — and listens carefully to what you as a parent have noticed. Remedies are selected for that individual picture and reviewed as your child grows and responds. Dr. Nafia's Post-Graduate Diploma in Counseling and Family Therapy also helps when school stress, sibling dynamics or anxiety sit underneath a physical complaint.

What parents should bring to the first visit

Bring any previous prescriptions and reports, your child's vaccination record, and notes on when complaints occur — time of day, season, foods, and anything that reliably makes things better or worse. If your child is on prescribed medication, do not stop it; bring it along so treatment can be coordinated safely with the prescribing doctor.
