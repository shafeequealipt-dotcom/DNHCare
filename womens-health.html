Treatments · Women's Health

Homeopathic Support for Women's Health, Hormonal and Thyroid Concerns

Hormonal health rarely announces itself with one tidy symptom. Irregular cycles, unexplained weight change, hair fall, low energy, mood swings, disturbed sleep — the picture is scattered, and women are often told each piece is "normal." At Dr. Nafia's Homoeopathic Medical Centre in Varthur, we look at the whole picture together and build an individualized plan around it.

Concerns we commonly support

  • Menstrual irregularities and painful periods
  • PCOS support
  • Thyroid concerns
  • Menopause and perimenopause
  • Hair fall and skin changes with hormonal roots
  • Stress, sleep and mood alongside hormonal complaints

A counseling-informed approach

Hormonal health and emotional health move together — stress disrupts cycles, and disrupted cycles add stress. Dr. Nafia (BHMS, Vinayaka Mission University) holds a Post-Graduate Diploma in Counseling and Family Therapy, which makes consultations here genuinely different: there is room to talk about what is happening in your life, not only in your reports, and that context directly shapes the remedy selection.

Working with your existing care and lab tests

Bring your recent reports — thyroid profile, ultrasound, hormone panels — and any current prescriptions to the first consultation. Homeopathic treatment is planned alongside your existing care: prescribed medicines such as thyroid supplements are never stopped or reduced except by the doctor who prescribed them, and progress is tracked against both how you feel and what follow-up tests show.

Common questions about women's health care

Can I take this alongside my thyroid medication?

Yes — prescribed thyroid medicine is never stopped on your own. Bring it along so treatment is coordinated and progress is tracked against your follow-up tests.

Will I need to repeat my lab tests?

Often, yes — periodic tests help track progress objectively alongside how you feel. Dr. Nafia will advise what to monitor and when.
