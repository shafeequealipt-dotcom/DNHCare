Good to know

Questions, answered.

How long does homeopathic treatment take to show results?

It varies by condition and individual. Acute complaints may respond quickly, while chronic conditions are typically reviewed over several weeks of constitutional treatment. Your doctor will set expectations at your first consultation.

Is homeopathy suitable for children?

Homeopathic remedies are commonly used for children. Dr. Nafia has specific experience in paediatric care and will tailor any treatment plan to your child's age and condition.

Can I take homeopathy alongside my existing medication?

In most cases yes, but never stop or reduce prescribed medication without consulting the doctor who prescribed it. Bring your current prescriptions to your consultation so treatment can be coordinated safely.

Do I need to follow a special diet during treatment?

Generally no strict diet is required. We may suggest avoiding strong flavours such as raw onion, garlic, or coffee immediately before and after taking remedies.
