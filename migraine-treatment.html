Homeopathic Migraine and Headache Care

People who live with migraine plan their lives around it — deadlines, travel, even family events carry a quiet "if my head allows it." At Dr. Nafia's Homoeopathic Medical Centre in Varthur, we treat migraine and chronic headache as the deeply individual condition it is, with remedies chosen for your specific pattern rather than a one-size prescription.

Triggers and patterns matter

Some migraines arrive with the morning sun and ease by evening; others start in the neck, or follow a skipped meal, disturbed sleep, screen strain or hormonal cycles. Aura, nausea, sensitivity to light or noise, one-sided pain — each detail narrows the remedy choice. That is why the first consultation focuses on mapping your headache history in depth.

Our case-taking approach

Expect a detailed 30–45 minute first consultation. We will often ask you to keep a simple headache diary — frequency, duration, suspected triggers, what relieves an attack — because a few weeks of honest notes sharpen both the remedy selection and the follow-up reviews. Stress and sleep are taken seriously here: Dr. Nafia's Post-Graduate Diploma in Counseling and Family Therapy is a genuine asset for headaches with a tension or anxiety component.

Realistic timelines

Chronic migraine rarely changes overnight, and we will not pretend otherwise. Treatment is reviewed over weeks, with the plan adjusted to your response. If you take prescribed medication for migraine or any other condition, continue it and bring the prescriptions along — changes to prescribed medicines belong with the doctor who prescribed them.

Common questions about migraine care

Can homeopathy reduce how often my migraines happen?

Treatment is planned around reducing the frequency and intensity of attacks over time. Realistic expectations are set at your first visit based on your history.

Should I keep taking my painkillers?

Yes — do not stop prescribed medication on your own. Bring it along so treatment is coordinated with what you already take.
