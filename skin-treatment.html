Homeopathic Treatment for Skin Complaints in Varthur, Bengaluru

Skin conditions like eczema, acne, urticaria (hives), fungal complaints and recurring rashes affect more than just the skin — they affect confidence, sleep and daily comfort. At Dr. Nafia's Homoeopathic Medical Centre in Varthur, we take an individualized approach: rather than treating only the visible symptom, we look at your overall health history, triggers, diet and stress patterns to select a remedy suited to you.

Conditions we commonly see

  • Eczema and atopic dermatitis
  • Acne and pigmentation
  • Urticaria and allergic rashes
  • Psoriasis support
  • Warts and corns
  • Fungal complaints
  • Dandruff and scalp conditions

What to expect at your first visit

Your first consultation takes 30–45 minutes. Dr. Nafia records a detailed case history — when the complaint started, what aggravates or relieves it, your past treatments, and your general health. Based on this, an individualized remedy plan is prepared. Chronic skin conditions are typically reviewed every few weeks so the plan can be adjusted to your response.
