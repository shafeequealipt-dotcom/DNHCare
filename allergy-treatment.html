Treatments · Allergies & Sinus

Homeopathic Care for Respiratory Allergies and Sinus Conditions

Bengaluru's mix of construction dust, traffic, seasonal pollen and shifting weather makes respiratory allergies one of the most common complaints we see at Dr. Nafia's Homoeopathic Medical Centre in Varthur. Sneezing spells that arrive every morning, a nose that blocks at night, sinus pressure that builds into headaches, or a wheeze that returns each season — these patterns wear people down precisely because they keep coming back.

Complaints we commonly see

  • Allergic rhinitis and hay fever
  • Dust and pollen allergies
  • Chronic and recurrent sinusitis
  • Asthma and seasonal wheeze support
  • Frequent colds and blocked nose
  • Food sensitivities

An individualized assessment of your triggers

Two people with "dust allergy" rarely have the same illness. One reacts at dawn, the other at dusk; one is worse indoors, the other outdoors; one gets sneezing fits, the other sinus pain. Your first consultation maps these specifics — triggers, timing, seasonality, family history and your general health — because the remedy is chosen for that complete pattern, not the label.

What a consultation involves

Expect a detailed 30–45 minute case-taking at the first visit, followed by an individualized remedy plan. Chronic allergic conditions are reviewed at regular intervals so the plan can be adjusted to your response. If you use prescribed inhalers or antihistamines, bring them along — nothing should be stopped without the prescribing doctor's guidance, and treatment is coordinated around your existing care.
