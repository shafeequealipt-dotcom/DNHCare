Understanding Monsoon-Related Infections in Children

During the monsoon season, children are more susceptible to infections due to the increased moisture and humidity in the air. This can lead to a range of health issues, from common colds and coughs to more severe conditions like pneumonia and typhoid. It's crucial for parents to be aware of these risks and take proactive steps to protect their kids.
