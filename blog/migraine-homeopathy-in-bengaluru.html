Understanding the Connection Between Monsoon Weather and Migraines

The monsoon season in Bengaluru can be a challenging time for people prone to migraines. The changing weather patterns, increased humidity, and fluctuating temperatures can trigger migraine attacks. Homeopathic treatment can help alleviate these symptoms by addressing the underlying causes of migraines.
